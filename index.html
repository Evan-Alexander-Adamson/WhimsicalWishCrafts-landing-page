Frequently Asked Questions

What are the calendar specifications?

Our calendars are printed on premium 250 gsm / 100 lb semi-gloss silk paper. The format is Ledger vertical (11" x 16.5"). Each calendar includes:

  • 13 sheets total (26 pages)
  • Two pages per month (image page + month view)
  • Cover page and back page
  • Double-sided printing
How does the calendar hang on the wall?

The calendar features:

  • Regular spiral binding (wire-O binder in metallic, black, or white)
  • Pre-punched hanging hole (positioned 0.57" from edge)
  • Unfolds to double size when hanging
How is the calendar packaged and shipped?

Your calendar will arrive in a protective flat packaging box (brown or white) to ensure it reaches you in perfect condition. We ship within 48 hours, and delivery takes 3-5 business days within the US.

What makes this calendar unique?

Our double-page layout gives you both a beautiful image and a spacious month view for each month. The premium paper quality and professional wire-binding ensure your calendar will look great all year long.
